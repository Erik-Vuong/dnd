Lich
AC: 13 (16 with Mage Armor)
Hit Points: 120 (16d8 + 48)
Speed: 30 feet
Initiative: +3
Proficiency Bonus: +5
CR: 17 (18,000 XP)

STR: 11 (+0)
DEX: 16 (+3)
CON: 16 (+3)
INT: 20 (+5)
WIS: 14 (+2)
CHA: 16 (+3)

Saving Throws: CON +8, INT +10, WIS +7
Damage Resistances: Cold, Lightning, Necrotic
Damage Immunities: Poison; Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing from Nonmagical Attacks
Condition Immunities: Charmed, Exhaustion, Frightened, Paralyzed, Poisoned
Senses: Truesight 120 ft, Passive Perception 17
Languages: Common plus up to five other languages

Legendary Resistances - 3/Day
Rejuvenation - A lich regains its body in 1d10 days. Before that time period, it becomes a demilich.
Turn Resistance - The lich has advantage on saving throws against any effect that turns undead.

Spellcasting - The lich is a 16th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Intelligence (Save DC 18, +10 to hit).
Cantrips (at will): Mage Hand, Prestidigitation, Chill Touch
1st Level (4 slots): Mage Armor*, Detect Magic, Magic Missile, Shield
2nd Level (3 slots): Detect Thoughts, Mirror Image, Misty Step
3rd Level (3 slots): Animate Dead, Counterspell, Dispel Magic, Fireball, Glyph of Warding
4th Level (3 slots): Blight, Dimension Door, Banishment
5th Level (2 slots): Scrying, Wall of Force
6th Level (1 slot): Disintegrate, Globe of Invulnerability
7th Level (1 slot): Finger of Death, Plane Shift, Delayed Blast Fireball
8th Level (1 slot): Dominate Monster, Power Word Stun

Actions
Paralyzing Touch: Melee Spell Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 10 (3d6) cold damage. The target must succeed on a DC 18 Constitution saving throw or be paralyzed for 1 minute. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

Legendary Actions (3/Round)
Cantrip: The lich casts a cantrip
Paralyzing Touch (2 actions): The lich uses Paralyzing Touch
Frightening Gaze (2 actions): The lich fixes its gaze on one creature it can see within 30 feet of it. The target must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw against this magic or become frightened for 1 minute. The frightened target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. If a target's saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the target is immune to the lich's gaze for the next 24 hours.
Disrupt Life (3 actions): Each non-undead creature within 20 feet of the lich must make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw against this magic, taking 21 (6d6) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
