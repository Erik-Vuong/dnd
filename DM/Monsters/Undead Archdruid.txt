Undead Archdruid
AC: 19 (Medium Armor, Shield)
HP: 105 (14d8 + 42)
Speed: 30 feet
Initiative: +2
Proficiency Bonus: +5
CR: 17 (18,000 XP)

STR: 12 (+1)
DEX: 14 (+2)
CON: 16 (+3)
INT: 10 (+0)
WIS: 20 (+5)
CHA: 15 (+2)

Saving Throws: CON +8, WIS +10, CHA +7
Damage Resistances: Cold, Lightning, Necrotic
Damage Immunities: Poison; Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing from Nonmagical Attacks
Condition Immunities: Blinded, Charmed, Deafaned, Exhaustion, Frightened, Paralyzed, Poisoned
Senses: Passive Perception 20
Languages: Druidic plus any two languages

Legendary Resistances - 2/Day
Turn Resistance - The undead archdruid has advantage on saving throws against any effect that turns undead.

Spellcasting - The undead archdruid is a 14th-level spellcaster. It's spellcasting ability is Wisdom (Save DC 18, +10 to hit).
Cantrips (at will): Druidcraft, Frostbite, Chill Touch, Thorn Whip
1st Level (4 slots): Absorb Elements, Blindness/Deafness, Entangle, Ray of Sickness
2nd Level (3 slots): Spike Growth, Hold Person, Misty Step
3rd Level (3 slots): Erupting Earth, Meld into Stone, Animate Dead, Bestow Curse
4th Level (3 slots): Blight, Ice Storm
5th Level (2 slots): Antilife Shell, Negative Energy Flood
6th Level (1 slot): Circle of Death, Harm, Soul Cage
7th Level (1 slot): Finger of Death, Fire Storm, Mirage Arcane

Fungal Body - Any critical hit against the undead archdruid counts as a normal hit instead, unless you're incapacitated.

Actions
Symbiotic Entity (2/Day): The undead archdruid gains 56 temporary hitpoints for 10 minutes.

Legendary Actions (3/Round)
Cantrip: The undead archdruid casts a cantrip
Symbiotic Entity (2 actions): The undead archdruid uses Symbiotic Entity
Spreading Spores (2 actions): The undead archdruid hurls spores up to 30 feet away, where they swirl in a 10-foot cube until the start of the undead archdruid's next turn. Whenever a creature moves into the cube or starts its turn there, that creature takes 33 (6d10) damage, unless the creature succeeds on a Constitution saving throw against the undead archdruid's spell save DC. A creature can take this damage no more than once per turn.
